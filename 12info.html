iPhone 12

The iPhone 12 is a flagship smartphone developed by Apple Inc. It was first released on October 23, 2020. The iPhone 12 comes with the following features:

  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display
  • A14 Bionic chip with next-generation Neural Engine
  • Dual 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras with Night mode, Deep Fusion, and HDR video recording
  • Face ID for secure authentication and Apple Pay
  • Ceramic Shield front cover for improved durability
  • Water and dust resistance (IP68)
  • Support for MagSafe accessories and wireless charging
  • iOS operating system with access to the App Store and Apple services

The iPhone 12 offers advanced features, including 5G connectivity, a powerful camera system, and a sleek design, making it a top choice for smartphone users.
