iPhone SE

The iPhone SE is a budget smartphone developed by Apple Inc. It was first released on March 31, 2016, and later updated with new models. The latest model, as of 2024, comes with the following features:

  • 4.7-inch Retina HD display
  • A13 Bionic chip with third-generation Neural Engine
  • 12MP Wide camera with Portrait mode, Portrait Lighting, Depth Control, and Smart HDR
  • Touch ID for secure authentication and Apple Pay
  • Water and dust resistance (IP67)
  • Wireless charging capability
  • iOS operating system with access to the App Store and Apple services

Despite its compact size and lower price point compared to other iPhones, the iPhone SE delivers powerful performance and a great user experience.
